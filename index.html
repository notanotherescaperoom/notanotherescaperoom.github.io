ABOUT

What's an escape room?

Escape rooms are a physical and mental adventure game in which participants must work with their teammates to find clues around the room and solve puzzles in order to escape. Escape rooms are great for corporate team building, family bonding, birthday parties, and fun group outings. The escape games require minimal physical activity and no outside knowledge.

ROOMS

Time Machine  

⏱90 Minutes   👥2-6 Participants

Rumor has it that when Calvin J. Lloyd died he left his nephew a time machine. Now, the nephew has gone missing and his family believes that he may be trapped somewhere in time. You have been hired as a group of investigators to look into this missing persons case to see if you can find the nephew and prove once and for all if time travel is possible.

You may encounter a live actor during your game but he will not try to scare you in any way. This game is not designed to be scary, however there are brief moments of darkness and atmospheric sound effects. Designers' Studio  

⏱65 Minutes   👥2-8 Participants

Fed up with making cutesy feel good rooms, the designers at Not Another Escape Room have created a room of their own. When they're told no one will be allowed to play their newest creation, they set up a bomb which forces you and your group to play their game, find the bomb, and deactivate it before it...well, you know.

Tell your friends you've signed up to do the "Unicorn Rainbow Room" and they will be shocked when they find out that instead they'll need to save the Designers' Studio.

There are no live actors inside the room with you. Apart from the simulated threat of an exploding bomb, there are no scare factors. Parties will have the option to choose between Average and Expert difficulties.
