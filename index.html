What We're Doing

  • We've placed a hand sanitizer station in the lobby and at various points throughout our games.
  • Thoroughly disinfect anything players may touch while in the building.
  • Wearing masks while interacting with players.
  • Washing and Sanitizing our hands between every game.
  • Ensuring no other groups are in the lobby before or after your game.

What We're Asking That You Do

  • Arrive between 10 and 15 minutes before your start time. Note that our lobby door will remain locked until 15 minutes before your scheduled game.
  • Wear your mask while in the lobby. You can take it off once you are in the game.
  • Sanitize your hands before and after the game.
  • Exit the building once you have finished the game and had a chance to debrief with us.
  • Let us know if there is anything we could do to help your group feel more comfortable.

ROOMS

Time Machine  

⏱ 75 Minutes   👥 2-6 Participants   Ages 10+

According to his family, Calvin J. Loyd has built a working time machine. Now that he's gone missing, the family believes that he may be trapped in time trying to right a wrong. You have been hired as a group of investigators to look into this missing persons case to see if you can find Calvin and prove once and for all if time travel is possible.

Children under the age of 14 must be accompanied by an adult participant. There are no live actors inside the room with you. This game is not designed to be scary, however there are brief moments of darkness and atmospheric sound effects. Please note that you will also come across the following piece of artwork while playing the game.
