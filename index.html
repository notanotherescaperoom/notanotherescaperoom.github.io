ROOMS

Designer's Studio  

⏱65 Minutes   👥2-6 Participants  🤔Average or Expert

Only have 2 people and don't want to pay for 3?
Book a game for Monday-Thursday using the promo code JustFor2

Fed up with making cutesy feel good rooms, the designer at Not Another Escape Room has created a room of his own. When he's told no one will be allowed to play his newest creation, he sets up a bomb which forces you and your group to play his game, find the bomb, and deactivate it before it...well, you know.

Tell your friends you've signed up to do the "Unicorn Rainbow Room" and they will be shocked when they find out that instead they'll need to save the Designer's Studio.

There are no live actors inside the room with you, apart from the simulated threat of an exploding bomb. There are no scare factors. Parties will have the option to choose between Average and Expert difficulties.
